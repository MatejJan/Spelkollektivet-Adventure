Entrance
Name: Entrance
Description: You find yourself between double doors that lead into Spelkollektivet, the indie game developers house. The way in is south into the lobby.
Directions:
    South: Lobby

Lobby
Name: Lobby
Description: The lobby is home to a few couches, a reception counter, and a computer with a VR headset plugged in. Shelves with dozens of shoes lean against the door. The paths forward lead south into the round Room, west to the dining Room, or back to the entrance doorway to the north.
Directions:
    North: Entrance
    SouthEast: Reception
    South: RoundRoom
    West: DiningRoom
    East: NorthWingEntrance

Reception
Name: Reception
Description: You are in the reception office that overlooks the lobby through a big window. This is where James (the founder) and Chris (the CEO) work.
Directions:
    NorthWest: Lobby

RoundRoom
Name: Round room
Description: You enter a spacious room of octogonal shape, somewhat incorrectly nicknamed the Round room. Couches are arranged into a makeshift theater, facing a big TV at one end. On the other, a waterfall wall decorates the space with pleasant sounds of dripping water. The only way out is back north to the Lobby.
Directions:
    North: Lobby

DiningRoom
Name: Dining room
Description: Rows of tables fill most of the dining room, a necessity to accomodate the 30+ game developers who live here. You may continue to the dishwashing room to the west or return to the Lobby.
Directions:
    East: Lobby

NorthWingEntrance
Name: North wing entrance
Description: You are at the start of a hallway that leads to the rooms of the north wing. Path further east leads to the south wing and there is a bathroom with a shower around the corner to the northwest.
Directions:
    West: Lobby
    North: NorthWing
    NorthWest: NorthWingBathroom
    East: SouthWingEntrance

NorthWing
Name: North wing
Description: The north wing is arguably where the cool homies live — you're lucky to be one of them. Your room is the one on the west side.
Directions:
    South: NorthWingEntrance
    West: YourRoom

YourRoom
Name: Your room
Description: You enter your room that greets you with essential Ikea furniture. It won't be long until this place will feel like home.
Directions:
    East: NorthWing

NorthWingBathroom
Name: North wing bathroom
Description: Bathrooms in Sweden, or at least at Spelkollektivet, are often simply rooms with a shower placed in a corner and a drain in the floor. A mop at the side serves for cleaning the water from the floor afterwards.
Directions:
    SouthEast: NorthWingEntrance

SouthWingEntrance
Name: South wing entrance
Description: At the east end of the building, the hallway turns south towards another cluster of rooms. The stairs lead down into the dungeon as the inhabitants of the basement offices lovingly call their working environment.
Directions:
    West: NorthWingEntrance
    South: SouthWing
    Down: BasementLobby

SouthWing
Name: South wing
Description: You reach a dead end, surrounded by homies' rooms on all sides. Since your room is in the north wing, you don't have much reason to be here.
Directions:
    North: SouthWingEntrance

BasementLobby
Name: Basement lobby.
Description: You enter a sizeable room without any windows. A few couches serve as a makeshift hangout space, although nobody is actually hanging out. You hear distant memories of ping pong balls echoing in the air.
Directions:
    South: LoudOffice
    Up: SouthWingEntrance

LoudOffice
Name: The loud office
Description: A long office space extends south with small windows helping the ceiling lights with illuminating the many desks. The place is nicknamed the loud office since conversations between inhabitants are allowed. There is even a green screen with a homie livestreaming from his computer. You feel like you'll fit right in.
Directions:
    North: BasementLobby
